# Warranty Policy

All laptops, smartphones, and TVs sold by the company carry a minimum
24-month manufacturer warranty, in line with EU consumer protection
regulations, covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions
under normal use.

Major appliances (washing machines, refrigerators) carry a 24-month
warranty as well, with an optional extended warranty of up to 5 years
available for purchase at checkout.

Accessories (mice, keyboards, headphones, power banks) carry a 12-month
warranty against manufacturing defects.

The warranty does NOT cover: physical damage from drops or liquid
exposure, damage from unauthorized repairs, normal wear and tear (such
as battery capacity degradation after 18+ months of use), or cosmetic
damage that does not affect functionality.

To file a warranty claim, customers must bring the product along with
proof of purchase to any store location, or initiate the claim online
through the customer support portal. Average repair or replacement time
is 10-15 business days.

Extended warranty plans purchased separately are non-refundable once
the 14-day cooling-off period has passed, but remain transferable if the
product is resold to another customer.
