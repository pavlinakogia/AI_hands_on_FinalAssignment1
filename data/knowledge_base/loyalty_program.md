# Loyalty Program

The company's loyalty program awards customers 1 point for every 1 EUR
spent, on both in-store and online purchases. Points are credited to
the customer's account within 24 hours of purchase confirmation.

Points can be redeemed at a rate of 100 points = 5 EUR discount on a
future purchase, with a minimum redemption threshold of 200 points.

The program has three tiers, based on the customer's total spend in the
trailing 12 months:

- Silver (default tier): standard point accrual rate (1 point per 1 EUR).
- Gold (spend over 1,000 EUR/year): 1.5x point accrual rate, plus early
  access to seasonal sales.
- Platinum (spend over 3,000 EUR/year): 2x point accrual rate, free
  express shipping on all orders, and a dedicated support line.

Points expire 24 months after they are earned if unused. Tier status is
reviewed annually each January based on the previous calendar year's
spend.

Loyalty points cannot be combined with "final sale" or clearance
pricing, and cannot be redeemed for cash.
