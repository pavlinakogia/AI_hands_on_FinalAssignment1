# Shipping and Delivery Policy

Standard shipping within mainland Greece takes 1-3 business days.
Shipping to islands typically takes 3-5 business days depending on
ferry schedules and weather conditions.

Orders over 50 EUR qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below
this threshold incur a flat shipping fee of 3.90 EUR.

Express same-day delivery is available in Athens, Thessaloniki, Patras,
and Heraklion for orders placed before 1:00 PM local time, for an
additional fee of 6.90 EUR, subject to courier availability.

Large appliances (washing machines, refrigerators) are delivered via a
dedicated logistics partner and include free installation and removal
of the old appliance, by default, within the Attica and Central
Macedonia regions. Other regions may incur an additional installation
fee.

Customers can track their order in real time through the order
confirmation email or the "My Orders" section of their account.

In case of severe weather (heavy rain, snow, or storm warnings),
delivery times may be extended by 1-2 business days; affected customers
are notified by SMS or email.
