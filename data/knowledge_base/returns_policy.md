# Returns Policy

Customers may return any unopened electronics product within 14 calendar
days of the purchase date for a full refund, provided the original
receipt or order confirmation is presented.

Opened products (laptops, smartphones, TVs) may be returned within 14
days only if the product is defective or does not match its description.
In this case, the company covers return shipping costs.

Accessories (cables, mice, keyboards, power banks) marked as
"hygiene-sealed" cannot be returned once the seal is broken, unless
proven defective.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business
days after the returned item is inspected and approved at the receiving
store or warehouse.

Products purchased during clearance or "final sale" promotions are not
eligible for return or exchange, except in cases of manufacturing
defects covered under statutory consumer protection law.
